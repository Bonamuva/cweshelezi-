About Cweshelezi Enterprise

Cweshelezi Enterprise is a proudly South African company dedicated to providing high-quality, affordable cleaning detergents for homes and businesses. From our bases in Durban (Ntuzuma) and Eshowe, we manufacture a range of trusted cleaning products including Pine Gel, Dishwashing Liquid, Bleach, and Air Freshener — all designed to bring freshness, hygiene, and comfort into every space.

“At Cweshelezi Enterprise, we believe that cleanliness is more than a standard — it’s a lifestyle. Our goal is to empower households and communities with reliable cleaning solutions that are both effective and affordable. We are committed to excellence, innovation, and service that reflects integrity in everything we do.”
— Miss L.M. Nxumalo, Director
